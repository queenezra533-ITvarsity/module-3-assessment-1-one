Smores

Prep Time : 25 minutes

Yield : 4

Ingredients







Steps

  1. Lay one tortilla on a clean surface.Scatter marshamllows into center
    leaving about about a 2" wide border around the center.Top
    marshamllows with graham crackers.Top graham crackers wisth chocolate.
  2. Tightly fold the edes of the tortilla towards the center,creating pleats.
    After wrapping quickly invert crunchwrap sp the pleats are on the bottom and they
    stay together.Repeat wth reamaining tortilas and ingredients to create more.
  3. In a large nonstick skillet over a medium high heat,melt about a tablespoon of butter.
    Add crunchwrap,pleated sidedown,and cook till golden brown,about 2 minutes.Flip crunchwrap
    and cook till golden on other side about 3 minutes more.
  4. Remove from heat and immediately toss in cinnamon sugar.
  5. Repeatwith remaining crunchwraps ,wiping skillet clean and adding more butter as neccessary.
  6. Cut in half and serve immediately.
