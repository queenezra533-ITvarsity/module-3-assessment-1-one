Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Prep Time : 10 minutes

Yield : 4

Ingredients





Steps

  1. Stir together butter and Parmesan cheese in a small bowl.
  2. Spread 1 1/2 tsp. butter mixture on 1 side of each bread slice.
    Place 4 bread slices, buttered sides down, on wax paper.
    Top with provolone and mozzarella cheeses; top with remaining bread
    slices, buttered sides up.
  3. Cook sandwiches, in batches, on a hot griddle or in a non-stick skillet
    over medium heat, gently pressing with a spatula,
    4 minutes on each side or until golden brown and cheese is melted.
