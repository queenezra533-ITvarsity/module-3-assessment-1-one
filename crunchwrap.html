Smores

Prep Time : 45 minutes

Yield : 20

Ingredients




Steps

  1. Grease a 9" x 13" pan with butter.In a large pot over a medium low heat
    melt butter.Add all but 1 cup of mini marshmallows and stir until melted
    and smooth .
  2. Remove from heat and quickly stir in Golden Grahams until evenly coated.
    Press into pan and top with chocolate pieces and remaining 1 cup mini marshamllows.
  3. Heat broiler, and cook until marshamllows are toasted,about 2 minutes.
  4. Let set at least 30 minutes before slicing.
